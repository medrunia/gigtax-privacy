Privacy Policy – GigTax

Last updated: March 11, 2026.

GigTax (“the App”) is an offline-first tax planning app for gig workers and freelancers. This Privacy Policy explains how the App handles your information.

1. Information stored locally on your device

GigTax stores data you enter directly on your device, such as:

  • payment logs
  • income sources
  • deduction entries
  • tax settings
  • filing status selections
  • notification preferences
  • locally generated reports

This information is stored locally on your device and is not sent to our servers. GigTax does not require account creation and does not use its own cloud backend to store your app data.

2. Advertising

GigTax uses Google AdMob to display ads. AdMob may collect and process certain information, such as advertising identifiers, device information, IP address, app interactions, and diagnostic information, in accordance with Google’s own policies.

You can learn more here: https://policies.google.com/privacy

3. Notifications

If you enable reminders or alerts, GigTax may schedule local notifications on your device, such as quarterly tax deadline reminders, weekly summaries, or inactivity alerts. These notifications are generated locally on your device.

4. Export and sharing

GigTax may allow you to generate reports or exports, such as PDF or CSV files, on your device. If you choose to share or email those files using your device’s apps, that action is initiated by you. GigTax does not transmit those files to our servers.

5. Data sharing and sale

We do not sell your personal information.

Because GigTax is an offline-first app with no account system and no backend operated by us, we do not collect or store your app data on our servers. Data may be processed by third-party services only where applicable, such as Google AdMob for advertising.

6. Data retention and deletion

Your app data remains on your device until you delete it.

You can delete locally stored app data by:

  • uninstalling the App, or
  • clearing the App’s storage/data from your device settings

Any exported files you create are controlled by you and may remain in your device storage, email app, or selected sharing app until you delete them.

7. Children’s privacy

GigTax is not directed to children under the age of 13.

8. Changes to this Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any updates will be posted on this page with a revised “Last updated” date.
